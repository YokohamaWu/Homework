1. 阅读（Reading）

主题： Diffusion and Osmosis in Cells
材料：

Diffusion is the movement of particles from an area of higher concentration to an area of lower concentration. It is an important process for living things, as it allows substances like oxygen and carbon dioxide to move in and out of cells. Osmosis is a special type of diffusion. It refers to the movement of water molecules across a selectively permeable membrane from a region of high water concentration to a region of low water concentration. Both diffusion and osmosis are passive processes, meaning they do not require energy from the cell.
