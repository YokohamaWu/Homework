Day 3 作业点评

Part 1: 阅读总结（Reading）

📄 作业原文

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants make their own food.
During photosynthesis, plants absorb carbon dioxide from the air and water from the soil.
This process is essential for life on Earth because it provides the oxygen we breathe and the food energy plants use to grow.

🔍 逐句点评 + 优化建议

句 1： Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants make their own food.

  • ✅ 用词准确、结构清晰，是教科书式标准定义句。
  • ✅ 使用了定语从句 by which，属于高分句型。
  • 优化： 保留，无需修改。

句 2： During photosynthesis, plants absorb carbon dioxide from the air and water from the soil.

  • ✅ 使用了介词短语 During photosynthesis 引导背景条件，很好。
  • ✅ 并列结构 absorb A and B 表达完整。
  • 优化： 可以更进一步增强语言的自然度：
  • 高分表达： ...absorb carbon dioxide from the air as well as water from the soil.

句 3： This process is essential for life on Earth because it provides the oxygen we breathe and the food energy plants use to grow.

  • ✅ 很完整的复合句，原因状语从句使用恰当。
  • ⚠️ the food energy 稍显生硬，可以更自然。
  • 优化建议： 改为：...because it produces the oxygen we breathe and the glucose that gives plants energy to grow.

📘 精讲与高分段落示例

高分范文段落：

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants produce their own food. It occurs in the chloroplasts, where plants absorb carbon dioxide from the air and water from the soil. Using sunlight as energy, these substances are converted into glucose and oxygen. This process is vital because it provides both the oxygen we breathe and the energy plants need to grow.

Part 2: 写作（Writing）

📄 作业原文

Photosynthesis is important because it provides the oxygen we breathe and the food energy plants use to grow.
Plants absorb carbon dioxide from the air, and using sunlight energy, to convert and oxygen.

🔍 逐句点评 + 优化建议

句 1： Photosynthesis is important because it provides the oxygen we breathe and the food energy plants use to grow.

  • ✅ 合理使用了 because 引导原因，很适合写作任务。
  • ⚠️ the food energy plants use to grow 稍显拗口，可换更自然表达。
  • 优化建议： ...and the glucose that gives plants energy to grow.

句 2： Plants absorb carbon dioxide from the air, and using sunlight energy, to convert and oxygen.

  • ❌ 语法不通顺，“using” 和 “to convert” 的结构不匹配。
  • ❌ “to convert and oxygen” 缺少对象，且表达混乱。
  • 优化建议：
  • 改为完整句：
    They absorb carbon dioxide and water and, using sunlight energy, convert them into glucose and oxygen.

📘 高分写作范例

Photosynthesis is important because it produces oxygen for humans and animals to breathe and glucose for plants to grow. Plants absorb carbon dioxide and water, and with the help of sunlight, they convert these into food and oxygen. Without photosynthesis, life on Earth could not exist.

Part 3: Combine Science 专项

📄 作业原文

Carbon dioxide, water and sunlight is the three main sources needed for photosynthesis.
Sunlight is used as an energy during the process, it turned carbon dioxide and water into oxygen and glucose.

🔍 逐句点评 + 优化建议

句 1： Carbon dioxide, water and sunlight is the three main sources needed for photosynthesis.

  • ❌ 主谓不一致，is 应为 are。
  • ⚠️ “sources” 一词在科学语境中不如 “components” 或 “requirements” 精确。
  • 优化建议： Carbon dioxide, water, and sunlight are the three essential components required for photosynthesis.

句 2： Sunlight is used as an energy during the process, it turned carbon dioxide and water into oxygen and glucose.

  • ❌ “as an energy” 应为 “as a source of energy”。
  • ❌ 两个句子之间缺少连接词，“it turned...” 是另一个句子，应使用连接词或断句。
  • 优化建议：
    Sunlight is used as a source of energy during the process. It helps convert carbon dioxide and water into glucose and oxygen.

Part 4: 听力（Listening）

📄 作业原文

Plants use chlorophyll in their chloroplasts to absorb sunlight and convert carbon dioxide and water into glucose and oxygen.
Carbohydrates are made of three elements, carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen.
Chloroplast contain chlorophyll, which captures sunlight and helps produce ATP and NADPH through electron transfer.
ATP is the “energy currency” made during the light reaction and powers the Calvin Cycle.

🔍 逐句点评 + 优化建议

句 1： ✅ 内容科学，表达清晰，是标准科学句。

句 2： ✅ 信息准确，适合展示知识点扩展。

句 3： ⚠️ Chloroplast contain... 应为 Chloroplasts contain...；句尾略长，可拆句。

句 4： ✅ 解释准确，术语使用得当，是高水平表达。

📘 优化后参考表达

Plants use chlorophyll inside their chloroplasts to absorb sunlight. This energy helps convert carbon dioxide and water into glucose and oxygen. Carbohydrates consist of carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen. Inside the chloroplasts, chlorophyll captures sunlight and initiates electron transfer, producing ATP and NADPH. ATP, often called the “energy currency,” powers the Calvin Cycle in the light-independent stage of photosynthesis.

✅ 整体总结与建议

  • 优点：内容覆盖全面，术语运用准确，阅读和视频理解能力表现出色。
  • 建议提升：在写作中注意主谓一致、句子连接、时态一致性等基础语法；拓展句型表达（如被动语态、非限定性从句、并列连接）可进一步提高写作得分。
  • 推荐保持每日练习 + 视频听力积累，并开始模仿高分范文段落进行重写练习。
