Day 2 作业点评

Part 1: 阅读总结（Reading）

📄 作业原文

Plant and animal cells share many similarities, like nucleus, cytoplasm, cell membrane, and mitochondria.
However, plant cells have a cell wall, a large central vacuole, and chloroplasts, which are not found in animal cells.

✅ 优点肯定

  • 结构清晰，先讲共同点，再讲不同点，符合阅读理解思路。
  • 使用了“However”引导对比，逻辑自然。
  • 科学术语拼写正确。

🔍 逐句分析

句 1：Plant and animal cells share many similarities, like nucleus, cytoplasm, cell membrane, and mitochondria.

  • 建议将 like 替换为 such as，更正式。
  • 添加适当冠词：a nucleus, a cell membrane...

句 2：However, plant cells have a cell wall, a large central vacuole, and chloroplasts, which are not found in animal cells.

  • 结构正确但略长，建议拆分。
  • 可替换为正面表述：Only plant cells have...

✍️ 修改建议

  • Use such as instead of like.
  • Use only plant cells to highlight contrast.

📘 写作技巧小结

技巧示例说明
使用 such assuch as a nucleus...更正式
强调对比Only plant cells have...句式简洁
总结功能These features help...突出意义

🏁 最终优化版本

Plant and animal cells share many basic structures, such as a nucleus, cytoplasm, cell membrane, and mitochondria.
However, only plant cells have a cell wall, a large central vacuole, and chloroplasts.
These unique parts help the plant cell stay firm and produce food through photosynthesis.

Part 2: 写作（Writing）

📄 作业原文

plant cells have a cell wall, a large central vacuole, and chloroplasts, which are not found in animal cells.
The cell wall provides support and shape for plants, while chloroplasts enable plants to carry out photosynthesis.
The vacuole stores water and helps maintain the cell’s structure.

✅ 优点肯定

  • 覆盖所有要点，结构完整。
  • 使用复合句、专业术语，表达地道。

🔍 逐句分析

  • 句首应大写，建议使用更地道的比较句式。
  • 个别表达可简化或换词增强多样性。

✍️ 修改建议

  • 用 “Unlike animal cells…” 开头。
  • 使用动词多样化，例如 replace “provide” with “give” or “support”。
  • 将被动复杂结构简化为自然表达。

📘 写作技巧小结

技巧示例说明
对比开头Unlike animal cells...逻辑清晰
动词多样化gives, helps, supports避免重复
使用自然句式make food更贴近表达习惯

🏁 最终优化版本

Unlike animal cells, plant cells have a cell wall, a large central vacuole, and chloroplasts.
The cell wall gives the plant cell strength and helps it keep its shape.
Chloroplasts allow the plant to make food through photosynthesis.
The large vacuole stores water and supports the structure of the cell.

Part 3: Combine Science 专项

📄 作业原文

Cell wall: provides support and shape for plants
Chloroplast: enable plants to carry out photosynthesis
Large central vacuole: stores water to maintain the cell’s structure.

✅ 优点肯定

  • 结构正确，任务完成度高。
  • 表达简洁准确，语言符合科学写作风格。

🔍 逐句分析

  • 建议使用完整句，加主语。
  • 注意动词单复数一致：Chloroplast → enables。

✍️ 修改建议

  • 将笔记风表达转为完整句。
  • 动词替换以增加表达多样性。

📘 写作技巧小结

The cell wall provides support and helps the plant cell keep its shape.
The chloroplast enables the plant to make food through photosynthesis.
The large central vacuole stores water and supports the structure of the cell.
